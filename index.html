The 2026 World Cup, in short

For the first time, the FIFA World Cup expands to 48 teams and is co-hosted by three countries: the United States, Canada and Mexico. It runs from 11 June to 19 July 2026.

The opening match takes place at the iconic Estadio Azteca in Mexico City, while the final will be played at MetLife Stadium near New York on 19 July 2026. Mexico becomes the first nation to host World Cup matches three times.
