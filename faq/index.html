Tournament basics

Core answers readers usually need first

What is WC2026?

WC2026 refers to the 2026 FIFA World Cup, the next men's World Cup tournament. It is the first edition with 48 national teams.

How many teams will play in the 2026 World Cup?

The 2026 World Cup will feature 48 teams, up from 32 in previous editions.

Which countries are hosting the 2026 World Cup?

The host countries are the United States, Mexico, and Canada.

How many host cities are there for World Cup 2026?

There are 16 host cities across the three host nations.

What is the group-stage format for World Cup 2026?

The 48 teams are split into 12 groups of four. Each team plays three group matches.

How do teams reach the knockout stage in 2026?

The top two teams in each group advance, and the eight best third-place teams also move on to the Round of 32.
