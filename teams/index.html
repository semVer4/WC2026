Favourites

Teams carrying the most obvious pre-tournament gravity

France

Built for late rounds

Depth is one thing, but knowing what a match needs late on is another. France usually looks like a team with both.

Argentina

Calm under tournament stress

Readers trust Argentina because the team often looks comfortable in the parts of tournament football that unsettle others.

Brazil

Ceiling as high as anyone's

When Brazil looks connected in and out of possession, it immediately becomes one of the most difficult teams to frame against.

Dark horses

Teams that may arrive just outside the loudest spotlight

Uruguay

Intensity and midfield edge

Uruguay often feels close to becoming a real problem because its matches rarely look comfortable for opponents.

Netherlands

Shape, width, and tournament craft

This is the kind of team that can become more convincing with every round rather than more exposed.

Japan
